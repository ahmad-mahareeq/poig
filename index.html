Who We Are

About POIG

POIG is a Palestinian ophthalmology initiative dedicated to advancing clinical research, medical education, and eye-health awareness.

We create a structured platform that connects ophthalmologists, researchers, and students to collaborate, learn, and contribute meaningfully to the field.

Our Vision

To build a simple, practical, and sustainable model for clinician-student collaboration in ophthalmology in Palestine.

Our Focus

What We Do

Clinical Research

Facilitate clinical research projects and academic publications in collaboration with ophthalmologists.

Medical Education

Organize structured case discussions and medical education sessions for students and professionals.

Research Training

Provide supervised research training for students to develop academic and clinical skills.

Awareness Initiatives

Lead awareness initiatives on preventable and inherited eye diseases across Palestine.
